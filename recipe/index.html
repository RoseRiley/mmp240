the greatest chocolate chip cookies

there are many great chocolate chip cookie recipes but this one right here is 1/1

ingredients

  • wash your hands (we're still in a pandemic)
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • set your oven to 350F
  • once you hear a "beep" noise go ahead and put your cookies in the oven
  • after placing those cookies in the oven let it sit for 12-14 minutes
  • once your are done grab a bowl of ice cream or a glass of milk and enjoy
